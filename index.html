About Me

My Introduction

I am Ezequiel Giampaoli, a Full-Stack Developer & BI Support Analyst based in Victoria, British Columbia, Canada. I combine a genuine passion for software development with hands-on experience in technical operations, data analysis, and web engineering.

Currently, I work at AML Oceanographic Ltd as a Fulfillment Technician & BI Support Analyst, where I build and maintain Power BI dashboards, create Epicor ERP BAQs (SQL queries) for operational reporting, and perform calibration and QA of high-precision oceanographic instruments. I also serve as Co-Chair of the Joint Health & Safety Committee, ensuring compliance with BC health and safety regulations.

My software background spans both frontend and backend: I've built web applications using Angular (TypeScript, HTML, CSS) and Python (Django), integrated REST APIs, and worked on full-stack university projects. I also have hands-on experience with WordPress, React, JavaScript, Node.js, and C#, gained through internships and personal projects.

My formal education includes a completed Diploma in Web Development & Digital Applications from ISPC (2022–2024), a year at UTN (National Technological University, Argentina) studying programming, and additional certifications through Oracle Next Education (ONE), Argentina Programa, and UBA's AI Multidisciplinary Training Program.

I thrive at the intersection of technical precision and creative problem-solving — whether that's debugging a complex API integration, building a responsive UI, or optimizing a SQL query for operational visibility. I'm always eager to take on new challenges and grow both as a developer and a collaborator.
